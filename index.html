Bash Love Letter


Oh, Bash…


Now that you’re terminal, I guess I should say all the thing that I wasn’t able to say before.


You always seemed so grounded, so aware of where you are, when you use pwd to return the present working directory.


You could always speak your mind and list exactly what was going on inside with ls.


And better yet, you always knew how to get exactly where you want by typing cd.


For example, there was the time you backed up with cd .., the time we went home with cd ~, and who could forget the time we went allll the way back with cd /.


I remember just how crafty with your hands. With a light touch, you could make a file. With a gentle mkdir, and entire folder.
